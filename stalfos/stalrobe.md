# Stalrobe

Stalrobes are skeletal mages, dressed in ragged robes, brought forth to aid armies of Stalfos. They use Rauru’s Shield to bolster an ally, and then blind foes with Stalrobe’s Veil. If disarmed of their staff, they may revert to using their own bones as a weapon, just as Stalfos do, making their Veil less painful (dealing 8 damage instead of 12).
