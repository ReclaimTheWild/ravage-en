# Stalhound

Stalhounds are the skeletons of war-dogs of yore, still clad in shreds of their ancient hide. They charge into battle recklessly, ripping past foes with incredible speed. Organized evil forces will sometimes use Stalhounds as mounts, riding into battle atop the bare husks of these hulking hounds.
