# Beamos

Beamos are a fearsome trap, their eye-laser punishing any dungeon-delver who steps within their small domain. If the room’s layout allows it, they can be run past very quickly. Unique to the Beamos, their eye is a Weak Point, the potent Light magic inside rendering it potentially unstable. If a Hero decides to destroy a Beamos, watch out for its explosive death throes!
