# Moblin Guard

Moblin Guards are bright-eyed, big-bellied lookouts for Moblin bases, patrolling during the day. In a fight, they like to rattle foes with a hearty Shield Clash to get their attention, and then use a Belly Flop to knock them all down in one go.
