# Moblin Firebringer

Moblin Firebringers serve as look-outs for bands of Moblins, using their lanterns to patrol or keep watch at night. 

They can throw Oil Flasks to drench foes or areas with oil. They might then use their Lantern to start a Firewall, or to catch their Boomerang on fire (a Minor action), to bring their fire to those oil-soaked foes.
