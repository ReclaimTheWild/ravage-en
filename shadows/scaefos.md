# Scaefos

Scaefos are humanoid figures, comprised almost entirely of darkness. Although they share the silhouette of people, they are incapable of making their own decisions.

Wisps of shadow flow off a Scaefos’ body like fog on a murky night. They use their magic to disable foes, and can slip into an ally’s shadow to be carried to more advantageous positions.
