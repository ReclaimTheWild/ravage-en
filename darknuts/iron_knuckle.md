# Iron Knuckle

Iron Knuckles are extremely durable warriors. They like to wade into the fray, and slowly grind their enemies beneath their sword and shield until they are vulnerable to a Fatal Blow. Be wary of attacking them from afar, as their skill at Deflecting is unparalleled.
