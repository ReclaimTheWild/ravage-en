# Zirro

Zirro are mushrooms with wings and stubby little feet. When angered, they dart to and from over the heads of their foes, dropping lit Bombs beneath them – and giddily darting just out of reach of any Hero who would try to engage them in melee combat.

The typical Zirro are most commonly found in desolate outdoor areas, such as ravaged fields, streams, and rocky hills.
