# Crow

Crows are wily birds, seeking out unwary travelers and swooping down on them to mercilessly harass them. Their goal is not blood or vengeance, but profit: Crows often try to Mug passers-by to relieve them of their goods, and then fly off with them to their nests. To a Hero, a Crow’s nest is as good as a Treasure Chest.
