# Horse

Horses are rarely found in the wild, in small herds that rove over hill and dale. Skittish and swift, a Horse is more likely to flee a fight than to attack. If tamed – or if rented or bought from a Stable – a Horse can make a valuable Companion, enabling incredibly swift travel and mounted combat.
