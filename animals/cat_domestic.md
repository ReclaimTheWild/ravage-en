# Cat, Domestic

Domestic Cats hunt small prey (such as mice and birds) by sneaking up behind them, and then pouncing, knocking them to the ground. If the first blow doesn’t defeat their prey, surely the second one will – if not, they may choose to retreat, lick their wounds, and try again when the heat is off.
