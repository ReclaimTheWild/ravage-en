# Big Moldorm

The Big Moldorm is, as one might surmise, a very large version of the standard Moldorm. Its tail is huge, bulbous, and incredibly sensitive to stimuli – sometimes, painfully so. When struck, it flies into a rage, slamming into foes with increased vigor.
