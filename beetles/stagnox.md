# Stagnox

The Stagnox is a fearsome forest beast, reigning over a lost temple or hidden glade, clad in a kind of cobbled-together Ancient armor. It has little patience for interlopers, and will almost always attack on sight. 

While on the ground, the Stagnox likes to try and charge foes with its horn, or to simply attack normally. While in the sky, it likes to Summon Blastworms on the foes below it, and then to try and charge in.
