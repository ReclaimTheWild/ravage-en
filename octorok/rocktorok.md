# Rocktorok

Rocktorok wear a stone atop their heads, and burrow into the ground to hide. Rather than striking from stealth, though, they prefer to pop up and suck in opponents. They then spit them out – sometimes, over cliffs, or just to slam them into a wall. They can also fire multiple stones in a single Bombardment with uncanny aim, raining rock from above.
