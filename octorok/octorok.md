# Octorok

Octorok are quick, agile snipers. They dart to and from firing rocks from their snoots at prey or passers-by. Though skilled with their natural weapon, and capable of firing long distances, they can easily be exhausted. If one can chase an Octorok down on foot for long enough without being pulverized by their rocks, they will be easy prey.
