# Brigand Hedge-Wizard

Brigand Hedge-Wizards aren’t all that capable as mages, but they can still cause Heroes plenty of annoyance. They like to light groups of Heroes on fire, block them off with quicksand, or – if forced to fight them fairly – hit them with a Keeta’s Touch, harming them and blunting their weapons.
