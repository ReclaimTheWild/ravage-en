# Aquamentus

Aquamentus are fearsome dragons, easily recognized by their single unicorn-style horn. They move slowly, and have stubby wings that allow them limited flight. They make up for their lackluster mobility with skill at breathing fire, so watch out for their flame breath and fireballs!
