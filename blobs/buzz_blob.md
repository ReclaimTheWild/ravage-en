# Buzz Blob

Buzz Blobs typically mill about, feeding on detritus on the ground and moisture in the air. They may not even seem to notice when someone attempts to attack them, letting their ever-electric nature do the work of warning the would-be predator off from a second attempt at harming them.

Nonetheless, on the occasion that Buzz Blobs may wish to attack, they are capable of (slowly) surrounding a target with angry electric globs of gamboling goo.
