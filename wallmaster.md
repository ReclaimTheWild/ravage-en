# Wallmaster

Wallmasters are giant, disembodied hands, lurking in dungeons and the dark, forgotten places of the world. They like to drop on top of foes, grab them, and drag them into the walls (hence their name). In a pinch, they can take more direct measures, too.

When a Wallmaster grabs a foe, they will attempt to drag them into the nearest wall or ceiling, squeezing them all the while. Once the Wallmaster has dragged a Hero into the dungeon’s stonework, it stops squeezing – because the Hero is unable to escape its grasp without being stuck in the stone forever. The Wallmaster then moves to the dungeon’s entrance, or some other pre-determined location, and deposits the Hero unceremoniously.
