# Zombie

Zombies serve as the lowest form of revenant undead. Though one Zombie is quite weak, they often come as a huge wave, crashing down upon foes and burying them in sheer numbers. If a foe wades into their horde, they will attempt to Grapple and put the Big Squeeze on them.
