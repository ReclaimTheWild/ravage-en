# Cheep Cheep, Common

Cheep Cheep like to lurk near the surface of the water, and hit foes with a spurt of water. 

Sometimes, Cheep Cheep will instead leap from the water and try to Strike foes. This can be quite surprising – but unfortunately for the fish, it’s much harder for them to get back to the water than to get out of it.
