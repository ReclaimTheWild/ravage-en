# Gohma Larva

Gohma Larva are the Gohma’s youngest, smallest form. Their eyes are still developing, making them incapable of firing the lasers they will one day be known for. Instead, they rely on their tiny pincers, using them to grab on to foes and weigh them down with their many tiny bodies – and hoping that something else can finish them off, delivering them their next meal.
